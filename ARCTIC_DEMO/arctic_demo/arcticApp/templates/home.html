Meet the Founder


Mr. Mohammed Insaf

The founder of Arctic, brings a wealth of experience and passion to the company. A certified fitness trainer with a deep commitment to health and well-being, Mr. Insaf has been immersed in the business world from a young age. His extensive travels have broadened his understanding of global business practices, fueling his drive to create a versatile and client-focused company.

Mr. Insaf completed his Bachelor’s degree in Islamic History from MES Kalladi College Mannarkkad, and his passion for fitness and health earned him recognition as Mr. Calicut University Champion in 2019. His diverse background, combined with his dedication to excellence, underpins Arctic's mission to offer exceptional services in work, study, travel, health, and beyond.

Meet the Co-Founder and CEO


Mrs. Sana Insaf

The co-founder and CEO of Arctic, brings a wealth of expertise in digital marketing and business management. Her entrepreneurial journey began during her college years, where she honed her skills and passion for business. Currently, Sana is further advancing her knowledge by researching international tourism management in Sweden, enriching her global perspective.

As the driving force behind Arctic's operations, Mrs. Insaf oversees the company's various divisions, ensuring seamless integration and exceptional service delivery. Her leadership and innovative approach are key to Arctic's continued success and growth.
